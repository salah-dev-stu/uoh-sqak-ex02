Con calls Klingemann's outputs **"novelty-by-permutation"** — a phrase doing all the work and proving none of it. Every point on a 512-dimensional latent manifold is a coordinate absent from the training set; "permutation" implies a finite lookup table, which generative models demonstrably are not (Olah et al., Distill 2017). Bender's "stochastic parrots" critique targets LLM hallucination, not visual originality — Con is smuggling a language-model argument into a GAN debate. "Aboutness" is anthropocentric goalpost-shifting: termites build cathedrals without blueprints, and we still call the cathedrals original. Ridler's labeling provided data; the bloom-temporality was unforeseen by her. Recombination at sufficient depth IS originality, regardless of substrate.
